About Us


Barangay Cugman Management System helps residents request documents.

The Barangay Cugman Online Request System is a digital platform designed to streamline and simplify how residents acquire essential local documents. Through this secure online portal, you can conveniently submit requests for a Barangay Clearance, Certificate of Indigency, Barangay Permit, and Certificate of Residency without the need to endure long queues at the physical office. By transitioning to an automated request form, Barangay Cugman aims to provide a faster, more transparent, and easily accessible public service experience, ensuring that your important documentation needs are met efficiently right from the comfort of your home.
